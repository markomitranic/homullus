Vincent Barré
sculptor

2014 | Website

This was a beautiful project and a big lesson for us. The main purpose of this website was to be a presentation for the French sculptor Vincent and serve as his online portfolio for years to come.

We received the beautiful design guidelines from the designer and translated them to HTML. Since Vincent has a collection of works spanning from the mid-70s, the main difficulty here was discovering the right way to do the navigation of the portfolio pages.

We decided that in order to finish the work and make it mobile-friendly (and tablet friendly!), we will need to use two axis navigation. Every row will be a year or a set period, and the X-axis will scroll by using a custom made jQuery animation and show the related thumbnails.

The work pages themselves were to resemble a book, catalogue or a monography. Unfortunately the project never lived to see the fruition and was scraped. But because of its beauty and lessons learned, we like to keep it in sight.
