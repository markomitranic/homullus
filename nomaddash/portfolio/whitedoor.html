Community

2012 | SMF Template | Live Chat | Yoomla Template

Remember internet forums? Neither do we. But these guys still use them. What can i say, they are in love with old tech, that is their mission.

So, two Italian friends had a great idea, god technical know-how and 0 promotional skills, so we contacted one of their moderators in order to offer our services.

Waking up the half-dead community meant diving into psychology of the group, making a new rank system, avatar rules, even kudos rules. Signatures had to be uniform, ranks had to be uniform, even the whole system of Admin-Moderator-Editor-User had to be rebuilt. We rebuilt their Yoomla website from scratch, created a new logo and a new Simple Machines Forum visual identity.

We discovered most of the visitors had a hard time finding out where the basic instructions are, so we rebuilt these. We even made a few instructables articles in order to promote the product to the DIY community.
