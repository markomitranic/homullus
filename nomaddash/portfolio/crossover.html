2015 | Website | Advertising

This was education, this was unlike anything we had done before. Optimizing copy, doing the SEO research, solving the unique issues visually, was a tough challenge for us.

Education is very tough for writing good copy. Our research had shown us that the reader has less than 4 seconds education promotional websites, so we have an extremely limited amount of time to get the message accross. You know who else has 4 seconds of readers attention? Highway bilboards!

So we made the home page to look like a bilboard, huge image, little text, just the basic data needed to take interest, and a call to action button!

The website is plain HTML based with a very simple back end for only changing the calendar dates of the future events. All the data that the user needs immediately is always in the right sidebar.

The website is responsive and has a cool drop down menu that pulls down from the top.
